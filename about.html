Our Story

Beauty Inspired by
Moonlight

Moonlight Étoile was founded in Mapo-gu, Seoul, with a vision to redefine luxury cosmetics. We set out to bridge the gap between rigorous dermatological science and clean, starry self-expression.

Each formulation in our skincare and cosmetics line begins with a single standard: to deliver absolute skin radiance without using filler ingredients, synthetic fragrances, or unnecessary irritants. We prioritize organic botanical complexes that soothe, nourish, and amplify your natural glow.

Whether you are locking down hydration with our five-ceramide **Starlit Cream** or buffing in our serum-infused **Aqua Skin Foundation**, Moonlight Étoile provides weightless comfort and beautiful durability that feels as light as moonlight.

Ingredients We Love

Niacinamide

Vitamin B3 · Strengthening

A powerful water-soluble vitamin that visibly refines uneven skin tone, calms redness, and strengthens the integrity of the epidermal barrier.

Ceramides

5-Complex lipids · Barrier Repair

Essential lipid molecules that bind skin cells together to lock in critical moisture, prevent environmental irritants, and restore elasticity.

Hyaluronic Acid

Multi-Weight HA · Deep Plumping

A natural humectant that attracts up to 1000 times its weight in water, pulling deep hydration into skin layers to smooth out fine lines.

Centella Asiatica

Cica Extract · Intense Soothing

A legendary botanical extract famed for its rapid wound-healing capabilities. Sourced to dramatically calm hot skin and micro-inflammations.

Peptides

Hexapeptides · Elasticity boost

Short chains of amino acids that act as building blocks to stimulate healthy collagen and elastin synthesis, firming overall face contours.

Vitamin C

15% Ascorbic Acid · Brightening

A superstar antioxidant that neutralizes environmental radical damage, fades hyperpigmentation, and yields a brilliant star-like glow.
