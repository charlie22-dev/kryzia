Our origin

Every ingredient
answered for.

Moonlight Étoile began in a small laboratory in Mapo-gu, Seoul, where our founder Yuna Bae spent three years reformulating what a "complete" skincare line could mean if you removed everything decorative.

The first product — our Petal Toner Essence — took eleven months and forty-three iterations. Not because we couldn't settle, but because we were waiting for data. Every formula passed independent irritancy testing before it left the lab.

Today our team spans Seoul, Berlin, and São Paulo. The rule hasn't changed: if we can't explain exactly why an ingredient is there, it doesn't stay.

What's inside

Hero
ingredients.

Bakuchiol

Babchi plant · India

A plant-derived retinol alternative that stimulates collagen without the photosensitivity or irritation associated with retinoids. Used in our Midnight Repair Oil at 1% concentration.

Used in

Midnight Repair Oil

Rosa Damascena Extract

Damask rose · Bulgaria

Steam-distilled from hand-harvested petals, this extract delivers a concentrated dose of antioxidants and natural humectants. We use cold-processed extraction to preserve bioactive compounds.

Used in

Petal Toner Essence

Centella Asiatica

Tiger grass · Korea

Madecassoside and asiaticoside compounds rebuild the skin's barrier through collagen synthesis. Clinically validated for reducing redness in sensitive and reactive skin types.

Used in

Petal Toner Essence Calm Peptide Serum

Ceramide NP

Synthetic identical · Germany

Bioidentical to human skin ceramides, NP (non-hydroxy phytosphingosine) rebuilds the intercellular lipid matrix. We use the pharmaceutical-grade fermentation-derived form, not plant-extracted.

Used in

Barrier Dew Cream

How we operate

The work
behind the work.

Post-consumer recycled packaging

All outer cartons use minimum 87% PCR board. Bottles are refillable by design — our Seoul refill station launched in 2023, with Berlin opening 2025.

Carbon-neutral shipping since 2022

We offset 100% of last-mile emissions through verified reforestation projects in South Korea's Jirisan National Park and Brazil's Atlantic Forest.

Full ingredient traceability

Every botanical ingredient lists its country of origin, supplier, and extraction method on our website. We publish supplier audit results annually.

No animal testing, ever

We are Leaping Bunny certified. We do not sell in markets that require mandatory animal testing, and we never will, regardless of commercial opportunity.
