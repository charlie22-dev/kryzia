Common questions

Quick
answers.

Standard shipping (3-7 business days) is available worldwide. Express (1-2 days) ships from our Seoul warehouse for most Asian destinations, and from our Berlin depot for Europe. Same-day delivery is available in Seoul through our courier partner.

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If your skin doesn't respond well to a product, contact us and we'll arrange a return or exchange — no awkward questions. Opened products are accepted if you've used less than a third of the product.

Most of our formulas are designed with sensitive skin in mind. Products marked "fragrance optional" are available in unscented versions. We recommend patch-testing any new product on the inner arm for 48 hours before full application. Our team of dermatologists is available for specific concerns via email.

Yes — we ship to 47 countries currently. Import duties and taxes may apply depending on your destination country and are the responsibility of the recipient. Our checkout will calculate estimated duties for most locations before you complete your order.

Our formulas are designed to work together. The recommended order is: Essence, Serum, Moisturiser, Oil. If you're using an active serum (like Sérum Lumière), apply it after the essence and before your barrier cream. Avoid combining the Midnight Repair Oil with any other active serum on the same evening until your skin has adjusted.
