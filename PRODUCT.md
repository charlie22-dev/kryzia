# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Skincare and cosmetics enthusiasts seeking premium, modern, luxurious, and clean products. They value self-expression, natural radiance, and highly polished experiences. They appreciate the aesthetics of high-end beauty brands such as ISSY Cosmetics, Rhode Skin, and Fenty Beauty.

## Product Purpose
The website serves as the premium e-commerce storefront for Moonlight Étoile. It aims to showcase their complete skincare and cosmetics lines under a beautiful, million-dollar brand identity, inspiring trust, confidence, and desire.

## Brand Personality
- Luxurious & Elegant
- Editorial & Spacious
- Bold & Radiant
3-word personality: Bold, Radiant, Elegant.
Emotional goals: Confidence, Radiance, Sophistication.

## Anti-references
- Side-stripe borders (e.g., thick border-left on cards or callouts)
- Gradient text and oversaturated AI-glow borders
- Glassmorphism as a default style
- Hero-metric templates and identical static 3-column card grids
- Pure black `#000000` (use off-blacks like `#1B1B1B`)
- Emojis in the user interface

## Design Principles
1. **Luminous Spaciousness**: Layouts must feel airy, editorial, and clean, utilizing generous margins and refined negative space to let the products speak.
2. **Tactile Interaction**: Transitions, hover states, and active pressed effects should react instantly and smoothly (e.g., subtle scale shifts on press, smooth image zooms).
3. **Cosmic Dark Palette**: Deep obsidian background with fixed starry starry skies, semi-translucent glass panels, and glowing starlight gold, rose, and neon violet highlights.
4. **Editorial Typography**: Luxurious heading typography paired with sleek, highly legible body text.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast-compliant text and UI elements over the dark starry background
- Meaningful semantic HTML, proper alt attributes, and keyboard navigability
- Reduced-motion consideration for page transitions
